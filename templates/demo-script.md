# Demo Call Script (30 min)

**Pre-call:** Pull 2-3 properties from their county. Check LinkedIn for their recent deals.

## 0:00–2:00 Discovery
"Tell me about your last auction. What county, what happened?"

## 2:00–5:00 Problem Framing
"How much time are you spending on research per property today?"

## 5:00–20:00 Live Demo
1. Enter real parcel ID from their county
2. Walk: BCPAO pull → lien discovery → ML score → max bid
3. Key moments: surviving lien kill, BID flag with ratio

## 20:00–25:00 Fit Check
"Does this match how you think about deals?"

## 25:00–30:00 Close
"$99/mo for your county or $399/mo statewide. 14-day trial — when's your next auction?"

**Objection:** "Too expensive" → "What's one missed bid cost you?"
