Log to Supabase `daily_metrics` table. Review every Sunday.

## North Star Metric
**MRR** — $2,500 by Day 90

|-------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Awareness | LinkedIn connections | 100 | 300 | 600 |
| Interest | Email subscribers | 50 | 150 | 250 |
| Interest | LinkedIn newsletter subs | 50 | 150 | 200 |

## Channel Attribution
- Direct (free tool signup): track `source=direct`
- LinkedIn outreach: track `source=linkedin`
- Referral: track `source=referral_[code]`
- Content/SEO: track `source=organic`

## Weekly Review Checklist
- [ ] MRR delta from last week
- [ ] New subscribers (email + LinkedIn)
- [ ] Demo calls held
- [ ] Conversion rate: free → paid
- [ ] Churn this week
- [ ] Top referral source
- [ ] One thing to improve next week
